Lasagna Recipe


Description

This is a better and less expensive lasagna you can make quickly

    Ingredients
  • 1 package of lasagna noodles
  • lean ground beef
  • sphagetti sauce
  • mozzarella cheese
  • ricotta cheese
      • Steps
  • Preheat oven to 350°
  • Heat large skillet and brown ingredients and drain
  • Put in casserole dish and bake for 45 minutes
