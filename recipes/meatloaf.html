Meatloaf

Description

A easy no fail recipe for meatloaf that quick to make

    Ingredients
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 cup of chopped onion
  • 1 cup of bread crumbs
  • Seasonings
      • Steps
  • Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  • combine ingredients and spread in baking dish
  • Bake for 1 hour
