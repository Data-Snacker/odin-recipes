Breakfast Casserole

Description

A hearty casserole that has eggs, susage and a blend of cheeses

    Ingredients
  • 1 pound of crumbled sausage
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 4 cups of shredded potatos
  • 9 eggs beaten
  • 2 cups of mixed shredded cheese
      • Steps
  • Preheat oven to 350°
  • Heat large skillet and brown ingredients and drain
  • Put in casserole dish and bake for 45 minutes
